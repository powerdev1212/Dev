  • TimeFrame

    The timeframe for rendering your online medical second opinion is typically 3 - 5 business days. Our triage nurses are meticulous at document preparation for the physician rendering the second opinion since this is a key piece in making sure that the most comprehensive report is provided to the patient making the second opinion request to Hygeiais.

    The timeframe may be delayed by a few days when any of the following circumstances apply:

    • If a pathology review is required for your second opinion
    • If the specialty physician required to complete your second opinion is not available at the time of your request or you have requested a specific physician to complete the second opinion and they are not currently available (note: the most appropriate physician specialist will be determined by our medical team)
    • Some complicated cases may benefit from our multi-specialty group and institute based model of medical practice. For cases in which multiple specialists may need to collaborate, an additional few days may be required.
    • Weekends and holidays are not included in the 3-5 day timeframe; strictly business days

    Our goal is to deliver a comprehensive second opinion to you so you can make an informed decision about your health or the health of a loved one. In the event there will be any delay in rendering your online second opinion, you will be contacted by the Hygeiais team.

    Please Note: If you send materials from outside the U.S., it typically takes three to five days for them to reach our office so please factor delivery time onto the turnaround time.

  • Notes & Referrals

    Once your consultation is completed, you will receive written notes from the doctor. We also offer referrals for top local doctors who accept your insurance and top institutions that are best equipped to carry out your treatment.
